About Us

Executive Assistants was formed in March 2011 by Alexandra Reynolds, who previously spent 12 years at Herbert Smith LLP, 9 years of which working as Managing Executive Assistant to the firm's then Senior Partner, the now Lord Gold.

Executive Assistants' purpose is to provide high level professional support services to small companies and individuals, who do not want the overheads of employing a full complement business support team, but who require all the support such a team would provide and more.

Providing service at the right level as and when required - working hard, delivering excellence, thinking imaginatively and adding real value.
